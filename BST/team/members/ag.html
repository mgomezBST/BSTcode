Amy Golden

Board Certified Behavior Analyst

Amy Golden is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) in private practice at Behavior Therapy Associates. She is also the Program Director of the HI-STEP Summer Social Skills Program in Marlton, NJ. Ms. Golden has been working in the fields of applied behavior analysis (ABA) and autism for over twenty years.

Ms. Golden has extensive experience designing and supervising home and school programs for students with autism, presenting on various topics to parents and professionals, and consulting in schools. Ms. Golden’s interests in the field of autism include teaching social skills, working with families to support their children using ABA, providing social and behavioral support in school settings, and promoting students’ independence.

Ms. Golden received her M.S. in applied behavior analysis in 2005 and became a BCBA in 2006.
