Stephen Clark

Vice President

Steve has a broad background in corporate and product marketing, and recently led marketing for Google's B2B travel products. Prior to Google, he was vice president of marketing at Convoq, Inc., a provider of real-time collaboration solutions. Previously, Steve was vice president of marketing for Allaire Corporation, a leader in Web application servers and development tools. He led the company's corporate and product marketing through its IPO and eventual acquisition by Macromedia. Steve joined Allaire from Sybase, Inc., where he was vice president of marketing and strategy for application servers and development tools, and vice president and general manager of Sybase's design tools product line. Steve holds a bachelor's degree from Brown University and a master's degree from MIT's Sloan School of Management.
